## 1. Introduction to NEXUS

Welcome to **NEXUS**, the premier AI-assisted mentorship ecosystem designed exclusively for university faculty and scholars. 

NEXUS bridges the gap between academic guidance and student execution. Whether you are a Faculty Mentor managing a cohort of mentees, or a University Scholar tracking your academic milestones, NEXUS provides a centralized, real-time environment for scheduling, collaboration, and progress tracking.

This manual provides clear, step-by-step instructions on utilizing the platform's core features to their fullest potential.

---

## 2. Authentication & Onboarding

### 2.1. Account Creation
To access the platform, you must register using your official university credentials.
1. Navigate to the **Sign Up** portal from the main landing page.
2. Select your designated role:
   - **"I am a Mentee"** for university scholars.
   - **"I am a Mentor"** for university faculty members.
3. Complete the registration using your `.edu` or official institute email address to ensure seamless verification.

### 2.2. Secure Access
1. Once registered, navigate to the **Sign In** page.
2. Enter your university email address and your password.
3. The system will securely authenticate your session and route you directly to your personalized Academic Dashboard.

## 3. The Scholar's Guide (Mentee Operations)

Upon logging in, Mentees are greeted by the **Academic Dashboard**, which provides a holistic view of upcoming sessions, task completion rates, and active mentor connections.

### 3.1. Establishing Your Mentor Connection
Unlike generic platforms, you do not search a public directory. Mentorship is formalized through official invitations.
1. Provide your **University Roll Number** to your designated Faculty Mentor.
2. When the Mentor adds you to their roster, a high-priority **Action Required** card will appear at the top of your Dashboard.
3. Click **Accept Connection** to officially link your accounts. You will now have exclusive access to their availability and resources.

### 3.2. Scheduling Guidance (Sessions)
The Intelligent Scheduler eliminates the friction of coordinating meetings.
1. Navigate to the **Sessions** module via the left sidebar.
2. You will see a calendar dynamically displaying the active availability windows defined by your Mentor.
3. Select an open time block and confirm the booking. The system will secure this slot exclusively for you.

### 3.3. Completing Academic Milestones (Tasks)
Mentors will assign specific tasks, projects, or milestones for you to complete.
1. Open the **Tasks** module to view your active workload.
2. Tasks are organized logically into `Pending`, `In Progress`, and `Reviewed` tiers.
3. Click on an active task to submit your work (via text entry or by providing a secure link to your deliverables).
4. Once your Mentor grades the submission, your Dashboard's **Task Progress** progress ring will instantly reflect your newly calculated success rate.

### 3.4. Real-time Communication (Messages)
For rapid guidance outside of scheduled meetings, utilize the **Messages** hub.
1. Click the **Messages** tab in the sidebar.
2. Select your Mentor from the active chat list.
3. Send text updates or inquiries. The platform uses a live communication bridge, ensuring your mentor receives updates instantly without needing to refresh the page.

### 3.5. Managing Excusable Absences (Medical Leaves)
If you are unable to fulfill your academic responsibilities due to health reasons:
1. Navigate to the **Medical Leaves** module.
2. Click **Submit New Request**.
3. Define the start and end dates of your absence and provide a formal reason.
4. This request is logged in the system, maintaining a transparent administrative record of your timeline.

## 4. The Faculty Guide (Mentor Operations)

Faculty Mentors are equipped with advanced administrative tools to manage their student cohorts efficiently and effectively.

### 4.1. The Command Center Dashboard
Your Dashboard is customized to provide high-level oversight of your mentees.
- **Active Capacity:** Instantly view the total number of scholars assigned to your guidance.
- **Task Success Metrics:** Monitor the overall completion rate of the tasks you have assigned.
- **Recent Activity Feed:** Track recent milestone submissions and finalized sessions.

### 4.2. Building Your Cohort (Adding Mentees)
You control your roster by officially inviting students to your cohort.
1. Navigate to the **Mentees** directory module in your sidebar.
2. Click the **Add Mentee** action button.
3. Enter the student's exact **University Roll Number** in the search interface.
4. Upon locating their verified profile, click **Assign**. The student will receive an invitation on their dashboard to accept your guidance.

### 4.3. Performance Analytics
NEXUS natively tracks and visualizes cohort performance.
1. Access the exclusive **Analytics** module.
2. Review interactive, real-time visual charts detailing your monthly Session Activity.
3. Monitor your "Performance Status," which intelligently highlights how efficiently tasks are being completed across your entire mentee division.

### 4.4. Defining Availability (Sessions)
Set clear boundaries and automated booking rules for your students.
1. Navigate to the **Sessions** module to configure your calendar.
2. Outline specific blocks of time you are making available for 1-on-1 guidance.
3. When a mentee claims a slot, the system automatically marks it as booked, categorically preventing double-booking conflicts.

### 4.5. Broadcasting Information (Announcements)
When you need to distribute structural updates or deadline changes to all your students simultaneously:
1. Open the **Announcements** module and click **Create New**.
2. Draft your broadcast and publish it.
3. The announcement will natively appear as a high-visibility banner on the dashboard of every single mentee assigned to your cohort.

### 4.6. Centralized File Sharing (Resources)
Eliminate email attachments by utilizing the Asset Repository.
1. Navigate to the **Resources** module.
2. Upload academic materials, grading rubrics, or reference PDFs.
3. These files become permanently accessible to your confirmed mentees, ensuring they always have the correct documentation.

## 5. Account Settings & Administration

All users must maintain accurate profiles to ensure the integrity of the platform.

### 5.1. Profile Management
To access your personal settings, click the **Settings** gear at the bottom of the navigation sidebar.
- **Contact Integrity:** Verify that your name and displayed email address are correct.
- **Professional Bios:** Draft a short biography detailing your current academic standing or research focus.

### 5.2. Role-Specific Mandates
- **Faculty Mentors:** Must define their **Areas of Expertise** so the system can appropriately catalog their academic jurisdiction.
- **Mentees / Scholars:** Are administratively required to provide **Parental Information** (names, occupations, and active mobile numbers) to ensure institutional compliance and emergency contact readiness.

## 6. Official Troubleshooting Guide

**Q: I sent an invitation to a Mentee via their Roll Number, but they haven't appeared in my active roster.**  
A: The Mentee must actively log into their own Dashboard and click "Accept Connection" before the system permanently bonds your accounts.

**Q: I received an "Offline" banner while navigating the platform.**  
A: NEXUS possesses active connection monitoring. If your local internet drops, the dashboard will warn you that real-time features are temporarily suspended. Functionality will restore immediately upon your internet reconnecting.

**Q: I am a Mentee, but I selected "Mentor" during account creation.**  
A: Role integrity is heavily guarded. If you selected the wrong architectural role upon registration, please contact your University IT Administrator, as accounts cannot freely swap between Mentor and Mentee permissions.
